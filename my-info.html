About

António Realista is a Lisbon-based director and cinematographer crafting visually-driven stories for brands, institutions, and digital campaigns. His work blends documentary sensitivity with cinematic polish — always hunting for honest moments, clean compositions, and purposeful light.

Recent projects span tvdocs narratives, institutional films, and performance-led digital spots. Comfortable from pitch to post, António collaborates closely with agencies and in-house teams to deliver clear, efficient productions.
